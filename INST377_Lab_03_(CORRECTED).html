Game Rules

You must choose either rock, paper, or scissors against your opponent. To add on, shoes is not at all used in this game.

The combos are as follows:

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Scissors beats paper
  • Paper beats rock
  • "Shoes" is omitted from the game
